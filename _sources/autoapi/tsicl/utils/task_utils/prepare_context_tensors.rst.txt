tsicl.utils.task_utils.prepare_context_tensors
==============================================

.. py:function:: tsicl.utils.task_utils.prepare_context_tensors(grid: torch.Tensor, series_c: torch.Tensor, covariates: torch.Tensor | None = None) -> Dict[str, torch.Tensor]

   Prepare context tensors for forward pass.

   :param grid: Raw time grid of shape `(bs, T, 1)`.
   :type grid: torch.Tensor
   :param series_c: Tensor of past values of shape `(bs, T, 1)`.
   :type series_c: torch.Tensor
   :param covariates: Tensor of covariates of shape `(bs, c, T, 1)`.
   :type covariates: torch.Tensor, optional

   :returns: **out** -- Dict with keys `coords_c` (context coordinates),
             `series_c` (context values),
             `covar_c` (context covariates, if any).
   :rtype: Dict[str, torch.Tensor]
